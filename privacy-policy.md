# Privacy Policy for YouTube Tab Switcher

## Introduction
Thank you for using the YouTube Tab Switcher extension. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Data Collection
The YouTube Tab Switcher extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The extension operates entirely on your local machine and does not interact with any external servers or services.

### Permissions
To function correctly, the extension requires the following permissions:
- `tabs`: To detect when you switch between tabs.
- `activeTab`: To determine the active tab and control YouTube video playback.
- `scripting`: To inject scripts that manage video playback on YouTube pages.
- `https://www.youtube.com/*`: To access and control video playback on YouTube.

These permissions are used solely to provide the intended functionality of the extension and are not used to collect or transmit any personal data.

## Use of Data
The extension uses the granted permissions to:
- Detect when you switch tabs.
- Pause YouTube videos when you switch away from a YouTube tab.
- Resume YouTube videos when you return to a YouTube tab.

No data is collected, stored, or transmitted by the extension.

## Third-Party Services
The YouTube Tab Switcher extension does not use any third-party services. All processing is done locally on your device.

## Security
We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. Since the extension does not collect or transmit any data, there are no data security risks associated with its use.
